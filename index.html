Who it's for

One account model, four vantage points.

R

Researcher

Owns a profile, tracks personal output, and builds collaborator connections.

IA

Institution Admin

Manages departments and oversees the institution's researcher roster.

RV

Reviewer

Evaluates submissions and participates in conference programme workflows.

SA

Configures the platform and audits activity across institutions.
